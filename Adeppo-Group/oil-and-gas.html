Fuel Oil and Vacuum Gasoil

TTOIL started out as a fuel oil and a VGO trader, and this is what we are really good at. Every month we move over 1 million tons of heavy feedstocks. Half of this is used by refineries to supplement their incoming crude streams, and the other half as blendstock for bunker fuel. Our fuel oil trading geography is extensive.

Middle Distillates

Middle distillates form the core of commercial road transport, industrial and marine fuels. TTOIL’s middle distillates portfolio serves industrial customers and retail operators in many countries, ranging from Europe to Americas and Asia Pacific. We cover the whole range of generic and special fuels – road diesel, jet fuel, low and high sulphur gasoil for marine and industrial applications.

Naptha

We are a major naphtha supplier to refineries, petroleum producers and other industrial users all over the globe. Our naphtha turnover has been showing consistent growth, reaching millions of tons annually. The product is sourced in the Former Soviet Union and from major Med producers and is taken to customers worldwide – from Europe to Asia Pacific.
